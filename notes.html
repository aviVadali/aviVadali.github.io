Generalized symmetries Draft

Higher-form symmetries: what they are and why they matter

Ordinary symmetries act on local operators. Higher-form symmetries act on extended objects — lines, surfaces. This changes what conservation laws look like and what it means for a symmetry to be spontaneously broken.
