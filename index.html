Condensed Matter & Quantum Information

I am a senior at Caltech working on theoretical condensed matter physics. Right now I think mostly about what happens to topological phases of matter when their anyonic excitations proliferate — and what that means for storing quantum information. I will begin my PhD at MIT in fall 2026.

I grew up in Chicago, IL. Outside physics I enjoy weightlifting, ultimate frisbee, reading, and generally being outdoors.
