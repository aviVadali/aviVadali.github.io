About

I am a senior at Caltech studying physics, with research interests centered on theoretical condensed matter physics and quantum information. At the moment, I am interested in topological phases of matter, generalized symmetries and anomalies, and out-of-equilibrium quantum systems.

Currently I work in the Jason Alicea group at Caltech, studying the stability of topological order to proliferation of non-Abelian anyons, and in the Ashvin Vishwanath group at Harvard, developing models for emergent symmetries in moiré systems. I will be starting a PhD in physics in Fall 2026.

In my spare time, I enjoy weightlifting, reading, playing video games, and walking a lot.
