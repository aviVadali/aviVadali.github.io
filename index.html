About

I currently work with Pablo Sala at UC Berkeley and Jason Alicea at Caltech studying the stability of non-Abelian topological orders to proliferation of anyons. I am also finishing a project with Tomo Soejima at NYU and Ashvin Vishwanath at Harvard on the Berry curvature produced by subjecting a spinor to a periodic potential.

In my spare time, I enjoy weightlifting, reading, playing video games, and walking a lot.
